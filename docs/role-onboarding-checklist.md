# OctoAcme Role Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist helps new team members onboard quickly by clarifying their role, responsibilities, and key collaboration points with other team members. Use this template to ensure consistent onboarding and reduce time-to-productivity.

---

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](./octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Review [Roles & Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) to understand your role and how you interact with others
- [ ] Get access to:
  - [ ] GitHub repository and project boards
  - [ ] Communication channels (Slack, Teams, email lists)
  - [ ] Documentation and knowledge base
  - [ ] Development/staging environments (if applicable)
- [ ] Meet your team and introduce yourself
- [ ] Review active projects and current priorities
- [ ] Identify your onboarding buddy or mentor
- [ ] Understand team communication cadence (standups, syncs, retrospectives)

---

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### Developer
- [ ] Set up local development environment
- [ ] Review coding standards and contribution guidelines
- [ ] Understand branching strategy and PR workflow
- [ ] Review CI/CD pipeline and testing requirements
- [ ] Complete a "good first issue" or shadow on a feature
- [ ] Meet with:
  - [ ] Tech lead or senior developer
  - [ ] Product Manager to understand roadmap
  - [ ] QA/Testing to understand testing approach

### Product Manager
- [ ] Review product vision, strategy, and roadmap
- [ ] Understand success metrics and KPIs
- [ ] Review customer feedback channels and research findings
- [ ] Understand prioritization framework
- [ ] Shadow user research or customer calls
- [ ] Meet with:
  - [ ] Project Manager to align on delivery
  - [ ] UX Designer to review design process
  - [ ] Data Analyst to understand metrics and dashboards
  - [ ] Key stakeholders and sponsors

### Project Manager
- [ ] Review active project charters and timelines
- [ ] Understand risk management and escalation processes
- [ ] Review communication templates and cadence
- [ ] Access project boards and tracking tools
- [ ] Review retrospective history and action items
- [ ] Meet with:
  - [ ] Product Manager to align on priorities
  - [ ] Scrum Master/Delivery Lead to coordinate facilitation
  - [ ] Developers to understand delivery capacity
  - [ ] Stakeholders to understand reporting needs

### QA/Testing
- [ ] Review test strategy and test plans
- [ ] Set up testing environments and tools
- [ ] Understand automated testing framework
- [ ] Review bug tracking and triage process
- [ ] Understand Definition of Done and acceptance criteria standards
- [ ] Meet with:
  - [ ] Developers to understand testing needs
  - [ ] Product Manager to understand quality expectations
  - [ ] UX Designer to review accessibility testing requirements

### UX Designer
- [ ] Review design system and component library
- [ ] Understand design tools and handoff process
- [ ] Review user research findings and personas
- [ ] Understand accessibility standards (WCAG)
- [ ] Review previous design reviews and feedback patterns
- [ ] Meet with:
  - [ ] Product Manager to understand user needs
  - [ ] Developers to understand technical constraints
  - [ ] Data Analyst to review user behavior data
  - [ ] End users (if possible) to understand pain points

### Data Analyst
- [ ] Get access to data platforms and BI tools
- [ ] Review data architecture and key data sources
- [ ] Understand existing dashboards and metrics
- [ ] Review data governance and privacy policies
- [ ] Understand experimentation and A/B testing process
- [ ] Meet with:
  - [ ] Product Manager to understand key questions
  - [ ] Developers to understand data instrumentation
  - [ ] Business stakeholders to understand reporting needs

### Scrum Master / Delivery Lead
- [ ] Review team's current agile practices
- [ ] Understand sprint cadence and ceremony schedule
- [ ] Review velocity trends and team health metrics
- [ ] Understand impediment escalation process
- [ ] Review retrospective history and improvement backlog
- [ ] Meet with:
  - [ ] Project Manager to align on coordination
  - [ ] Product Manager to understand backlog readiness
  - [ ] All team members to understand current challenges

### Stakeholder (Business Sponsor, Support, Sales)
- [ ] Review project charter and business objectives
- [ ] Understand your engagement pattern and communication cadence
- [ ] Review stakeholder reporting format and schedule
- [ ] Identify your primary points of contact (PM, PdM)
- [ ] Understand escalation and decision-making process
- [ ] Meet with:
  - [ ] Project Manager for status updates
  - [ ] Product Manager for product direction

---

## First 30 Days Goals

### Week 1
- Complete general onboarding checklist
- Attend all team meetings and ceremonies
- Shadow team members in your role

### Week 2
- Complete role-specific onboarding
- Take on your first assignment with support
- Provide feedback on onboarding process

### Week 3-4
- Work more independently with check-ins
- Contribute to team discussions and planning
- Identify one process improvement opportunity

---

## Onboarding Feedback

After 30 days, provide feedback to help improve this process:
- What was most helpful during onboarding?
- What information was missing or unclear?
- What would you change about the onboarding process?
- How long did it take you to feel productive in your role?
